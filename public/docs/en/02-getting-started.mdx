# Getting Started with Beryx

Before we dive into each feature, let's get you started with Beryx. Follow these steps:

**Step 1: Explore the Homepage**
Begin your journey at [beryx.zondax.ch](https://beryx.zondax.ch).

**Step 2: Easy Search**
Central to the homepage is the **Search Bar**, where you can look up transactions, accounts, smart contracts, or tipsets. Not sure what to search for? Check out the examples below the search bar.

**Step 3: Sidebar Navigation**
On the left, a sidebar acts like a menu, offering a suite of pages and tools:
- **Recent Activity**: [here](https://beryx.zondax.ch/recent_activity)
- **Network Insights**: [here](https://beryx.zondax.ch/dashboard)
- **Address Converter**: [here](https://beryx.zondax.ch/address_converter)
- **Mempool Monitor**: [here](https://beryx.zondax.ch/mempool)
- **Calibration Faucet**: [here](https://beryx.zondax.ch/faucet)
- **Smart Contract Safety**: [here](https://beryx.zondax.ch/contract_verifier)

**Step 4: Top Bar Features**
Look up to find the top bar, which includes:
- **Network Selector**: Choose the network for your search.
- **Filecoin Price**: Stay updated with the latest price.
- **Latest Tipset**: Get quick access to the most recent tipset.
- **App Notifications**: Receive important updates and alerts.
- **Settings**: Customize your Beryx experience.
- **Wallet Connection**: Connect your wallet or track addresses directly from the top bar.
